Privacy Policy for Skybridge

Key Points:
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is collected or transmitted to our servers
  • Your Bluesky credentials never leave your device

Data Storage

Skybridge stores only the following information locally on your device:

  • Your Bluesky identifier (handle or email)
  • Your Bluesky app password

This information is required for the basic functionality of cross-posting from X (Twitter) to Bluesky. The app password is stored securely in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted anywhere except to Bluesky's servers when posting content.

Data Collection

Skybridge does not:

  • Collect any analytics or usage data
  • Use any tracking mechanisms
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Store any data on external servers

Data Transmission

The only data transmission that occurs is:

  • Direct communication between your browser and Bluesky's servers when posting content
  • Reading content from X (Twitter) to cross-post to Bluesky

Your Control

You can remove your data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's storage for this extension
  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Revoking the app password from your Bluesky account

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Skybridge's handling of your data, please contact us at [Your Contact Information].
